Assignment Innovation

This assignment replaces the traditional research paper format with an interactive web-based presentation. You will perform the same rigorous financial analysis but communicate your findings through a modern digital medium that reflects contemporary business communication standards.

Executive Summary

You will conduct a comprehensive financial ratio analysis of Black Knight, Inc., evaluating liquidity, leverage, asset efficiency, and profitability metrics against industry benchmarks. Rather than submitting a conventional paper, you will develop a professional website that presents your analysis in an accessible, visually compelling format using generative AI tools.

This approach develops both your analytical capabilities and your proficiency in digital communication—skills increasingly essential in modern finance and consulting roles. The final deliverable should demonstrate the depth of insight expected in professional financial analysis while leveraging contemporary presentation methods.

Website Components

Your analysis website must integrate six core sections:

  1. 1. Title Section: Company identification, assignment context, student attribution, fiscal year, industry classification
  2. 2. Company Profile: Business model overview, industry positioning, 2022 performance highlights, material events affecting results
  3. 3. Ratio Dashboard: All calculated metrics displayed with industry comparisons and visual performance indicators
  4. 4. Strategic Analysis: Detailed interpretation of each ratio category with business implications (1,000+ words total)
  5. 5. Executive Assessment: Synthesis of findings, strategic recommendations, investment perspective
  6. 6. References: Minimum five scholarly sources in APA format, including the 10-K filing

Analytical Framework

For each ratio category, your analysis should address:

  • Interpretation: What does this metric reveal about the firm's financial position?
  • Benchmarking: How does performance compare to industry standards?
  • Implications: What strategic concerns or competitive advantages emerge?
  • Context: How do broader business conditions inform these results?
